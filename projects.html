Scroll through the five stages behind the Cobra—each one turning an idea into a safer, faster, more complete electric race car.

01
Define the mission

Design + planning

Set performance targets, study EVGP requirements, choose parts, and turn constraints into a buildable layout.

  • Competition rules
  • Component selection
  • Safety planning
02
Make it physical

Build + assembly

Fit the frame, steering, seat, wheels, controls, and mechanical systems into one working machine.

  • Chassis assembly
  • Driver ergonomics
  • Mechanical fit
03
Bring it to life

Wiring + controls

Connect batteries, controller, motor, kill switch, and driver inputs into an organized 48V system.

  • Power distribution
  • Control wiring
  • Safe shutdown
04
Find the weak points

Testing + troubleshooting

Run the car, observe its behavior, diagnose failures, and record what must change before the next session.

  • Functional tests
  • Driver feedback
  • Fault diagnosis
05
Prepare to compete

Final adjustments

Refine balance, reliability, safety, and presentation so the car and team are ready for EVGP.

  • Weight balance
  • Reliability checks
  • Race preparation
