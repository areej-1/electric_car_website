05 / Engineering
Problems become progress

What we learn when the first idea fails.

Challenge

Weight balance

Uneven weight can change the way the car turns and behaves at speed.

Our response

Test component placement and adjust the balance before performance runs.

Challenge

Reliable wiring

Loose or unclear connections make troubleshooting slower and less safe.

Our response

Organize, secure, label, and verify the power system one circuit at a time.

Challenge

Driver protection

Performance only matters when the driver and crew can operate safely.

Our response

Use a kill switch, seat belt, helmet, and repeatable safety checks.
