05 / Engineering
Problems become progress

What we learn when the first idea fails.

Weight balance
Problem

Uneven weight can change how the car turns and behaves at speed.

Decision

Mechanics shifted battery placement after low-speed runs.

Result / next test

Weight remains near 186 kg; a new weigh-in is pending after the next parts change.

Reliable wiring
Problem

Loose or unclear connections make troubleshooting slower and less safe.

Decision

The electrical crew re-checked the 48V layout, secured connectors, and re-labeled critical runs.

Result / next test

The next test day has a clearer, faster troubleshooting path.

Driver protection
Problem

Performance only matters when the driver and crew can operate safely.

Decision

Use an emergency kill switch, seat belt, helmet, and repeatable pre-run checks.

Result / next test

The hardware is documented; final race-day safety sign-off is still tracked in the checklist.
